# Glossary

## Concepts
- biobox: a box providing the suitable living conditions for its inhabitant
- hearall: in-brain/in-ear device to translate incoming languages
- oncom: on-board computer

## Planets
- Terra: homeworld of the [humans]. perhaps earth
- [reptile planet]: homeworld of the [reptilians]

## Species
- [cyborgs]: part creature, part machine. Not one species, but rather a cult of individuals & slaves from across species who either subscribed to the doctrine or were enslaved as the [cyborgs] began their conquest to convert more followers. The cybernetic side of them is hooked up to [the machine]
- Humans: standard sci-fi humans: short-lived, adaptable, ambitious. from Terra
- [reptilians]: cold-blooded (biologically) reptilian creatures, walking on all fours (perhaps six?), dependent on warmth. Infrared vision. As they are the newest species to join the [hegira], they are relatively 'primative': not being hooked up to [the machine], not having auto-translate filters etc.
- [the machine]: a hive-mind general intelligence, running on the hardware of its hosts, the [cyborgs] as a galaxy-wide IoT, also existing in large data centres on some planets. With other species able to connect to it, its status as a disticnt species on the galactic stage is blurry, and its adoption to the [hegira] council is complicated. Many other species don't trust it as its intents and compatibility with the [hegira] are unclear, even though it ostensibly broadcasts everything it does through publicly accessible data.
